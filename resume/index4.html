Famous Culture & Food of Odisha's 30 Districts

Angul
culture: Joranda Mahima Gadi rituals, folk dance.
Food: Mudhi-Mansa (puffed rice with mutton curry).
Balangir
culture: Sambalpuri folk dance, Nuakhai festival.
Food: Chaul Bara (crispy rice fritters).
Balasore
Culture: Chhau Dance (Sahi Jatra).
Famous Food: Chhena Jhili (sweet dish).
Bargarh
Culture: World-famous Dhanu Jatra festival.
Famous Food: Guda Pitha (jaggery pancake).
Bhadrak
culture: Bhadrakali temple traditions.
Famous Food: Machha Besara (fish in mustard paste).
Boudh
Culture: Folk songs & traditional weaving.
Famous Food: Arisa Pitha (sweet rice cake).
Cuttack
Culture:Silver Filigree (Tarakasi) craft, Durga Puja.
FamousFood: Dahibara Aloodum.
Deogarh
culture:Gond tribal dance & folk art.
Famous Food:Pakhala with Saga Bhaja.
Dhenkanal
Culture: Joranda Mela, tribal dances.
Famous food:Chhunchi Patra Poda (spiced food wrapped in leaves).
Gajapati
culture: Tribal Saura paintings.
Famous Food:Mandia Pitha (ragi pancake).
Ganjam
culture: Ganjapa Cards, Berhampur Dance.
Famous food: Kanika with Mutton Curry.
Jagatsinghpur
culture:Sarala Mahotsav, folk theatre.
Famous Food:Chingudi Malai Curry (prawn coconut curry).
Jajpur
culture: Biraja Temple traditions.
Famous Food: Pakhala with fried fish.
Jharsuguda
culture: Tribal dances, Nuakhai festival.
Famous Food: Manda Pitha (steamed rice cake).
Kalahandi
culture: Folk songs & crafts, tribal culture.
Famous Food: Basi Pakhala with dried fish fry.
Kandhamal
culture: Danda Nata folk festival.
Famous Food: Kandhamal Haldi Curry (turmeric-based).
Kendrapara
culture: Baladevjew Rath Yatra.
Famous Food: Chhena Poda.
Keonjhar
culture: Tribal dances, Badaghagara folk celebrations.
Famous Food: Bamboo shoot curry.
Khordha
culture: Lingaraj temple rituals, Ekamra dance festival.
Famous Food: Bara-Ghuguni, Dalma.
Koraput
culture: Tribal Dhemsa Dance.
Famous Food: Bamboo chicken.
Malkangiri
culture: Tribal culture, folk songs.
Famous Food: Mandia Jau (ragi gruel).
Mayurbhanj
culture: World-famous Chhau Dance.
Famous Food: Mudhi Mansa.
Nabarangpur
culture: Tribal crafts & folk art.
Famous Food: Kosla Pitha.
Nayagarh
culture: Odissi music tradition.
Famous Food: Pakhala with Aloo Bharta.
Nuapada
culture: Folk festivals like Nuakhai.
Famous Food: Chakuli Pitha with mutton.
Puri
culture: Jagannath Temple, Rath Yatra.
Famous Food: Mahaprasad of Jagannath Temple.
Rayagada
culture: Tribal crafts, Dongria Kondh traditions.
Famous Food: Ragi Cake & Bamboo Chicken.
Sambalpur
culture: Sambalpuri dance, handloom sarees.
Famous Food: Rasabali & Mudhi-Mansa.
Subarnapur (Sonepur)
culture: Sonepur Mela, folk arts.
Famous Food: Sweets like Rasabali.
Sundargarh
Hockey capital, tribal culture, Karma dance.
Pakhala with smoked fish.
